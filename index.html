About the Artist

Saumya Asthana

"I create art that feels personal, elegant, and timeless — every brushstroke is layered with intention."

Hi, I'm Saumya, a visual artist with a Master's in Fine Arts and a deep love for storytelling through paint. Based in Bangalore, I have over 7 years of experience in portraiture, specializing in hyper-realistic pencil portraits, expressive watercolor paintings, vibrant oil-on-canvas works, and Live Wedding Paintings that capture the essence of your big day — while it's happening.

From intimate pencil portraits that reveal every texture and nuance, to miniature watercolor landscapes, to majestic mythological paintings — my versatility spans across mediums and subjects. Every piece I create is meant to evoke emotion, not just mimic a photo.
